To work on vi editor
write vi <file_name>
after the editor opens press i or a - without doing that you can't type anything
i- insert
a- append
type what info you want to enter in vi editor . After you are done with it

 press esc button . it will take you out from insert/append mode

then press colon(:) and wq <:wq> it is to  save the file and quit.

when you press the colon it will take you to the bottom of the page.
